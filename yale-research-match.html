YCC Tech · Developer Prize

Student-built research infrastructure for Yale, not just another app.

Yale students are already told to "just email professors," but the path is opaque and uneven. This student-built tool acts like a discovery layer for Yale labs: search the way students actually think—proteomics, cancer, aging, neuro, wet lab, computational—and get concrete matches, prerequisites, and outreach-ready next steps.
